Project Idea

We will be developing a website that targets users who need to move their stuff inter-city or out of city Our website will provide them with moving trucks on demand at standard discounted rates.

Services:

Saving Time:

Our website will save users their time spent on finding someone who will move their luggage

Cost Saving:

Our website will provide standard discounted rates according to the market.

Less Headache:

Our website will save users the headache of figuring out where to find a mover.

Expandability:

In addition to moving out of city, our website will also provide services for moving luggage inter-city.

Inter-City Moving:

In addition to moving individual luggage inter-city, we will provide to move complete luggage from one house to another in the same city.

Luggage Tracking:

Our website will also provide luggage tracking to users.

Luggage Size:

Users can choose trucks depending on their luggage size.

Luggage Security:

Movers will be rated so that users can know who is moving their luggage and feel secure.

Extra Features:

• User-Experience will be kept in mind.
• UI will be kept as simple as possible.
• Responsive Design.
• Fast and bugs free.
• English and Urdu both will be available for easy usability for some users.
• User reviews to rate movers
